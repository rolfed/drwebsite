About

Hi. I'm Danniel S. Rolfe

I'm a software developer based in Hillsboro, Oregon. The focus of my work is building web applications with open-source technologies. I pried myself in developing code that is maintainable, organized and tested.

Currently, I'm contracting at The Standard. I'm part of the Core Engineering team who's focus is to move the compony off outdated legacy web technologies onto an open-source stack.

My background includes over five years of software development experience. Some of the notable companies I have worked on projects includes Hewlett-Packard, Sitka Center for Art and Ecology, 17Hats, Manifest, Oregon Catholic Press, The Standard and more.

Take some time to look at my work, credentials and if you have a project that I can contribute to, let's get in touch.
